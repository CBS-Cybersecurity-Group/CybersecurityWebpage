Projects

An overview of all the current projects.

The Danish Cybersecurity Championship

CTF development and workshops

The Cybersecurity Lab at CBS develops CTF challenges for the Danish Cybersecurity Championship. Additionally, workshops on data analysis with R and an introduction to the command line are provided.

Cybersecurity Club

Building a cybersecurity community

The Cybersecurity Club aims to introduce students to cybersecurity by hosting events connecting students, researchers, and professionals.
